Inheritance

Both h2 and p elements inherit color from their parent, the section element.

Most specific

Only the h2 element inherits color from its parent, the section element.
The p element is now specific.

!important

Noticing the colour of the h2 element, here is where the !important rule applied.
As you can see, !important also follows the latest-win rule.

Latest-win

Noticing the colour of the h2 element, here is where the latest-win rule applied (i.e., the latest will override previous ones).
You may wonder about the order of the inheritance and the latest-win classes in the class attribute of this section.
Looking at the CSS tab content, you can recognise that the order of the rules is in the stylesheet section/file, not in the class attribute.
